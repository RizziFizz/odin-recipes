Turkey Tails

I usually make this dish along side my brother with making a tomato soup as it combos really well this turkey cooking style can be applied to any type of turkey I just prefer tails

What do you need

    Avacado oil Turkey tails melted Butter Salt Pepper Garlic Powder dried thyme paprika

Instructions

    first get your butter and dump it on the turkeys then mix all your seasons together and coat your turkeys with them, after that put them in your oven after setting it to 450 after waiting 20 minutes lightly cover your turkey tails with some foil and put them back in finally take them out 30 minutes later and serve them after they have cooled down
